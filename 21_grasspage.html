Grass Type Pokemon


  • Description: Grass-type Pokémon are often plant-based creatures with moves related to nature, plants, and sometimes poison. They are known for their vibrant designs and supportive moves.
  • Strength: Grass types are effective against Water, Ground, and Rock Pokémon, using their botanical powers to overcome these types.
  • Weakness: They are, however, vulnerable to Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug types, as fire can scorch plants, and these types can exploit weaknesses in their structure.
  • Best Starter Pokémon: Bulbasaur, a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon, evolves into Ivysaur and later into Venusaur, combining the Grass type with poison capabilities.
