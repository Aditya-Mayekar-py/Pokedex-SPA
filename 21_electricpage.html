Electric Type Pokemon


  • Description: Electric-type Pokémon generate and control electricity. They are known for their high speed and often feature moves that can paralyze opponents.
  • Strength: Electric types are strong against Water and Flying Pokémon, as water conducts electricity, and flying types can be struck down by lightning.
  • Weakness: However, they are vulnerable to Ground types, as the ground neutralizes electric attacks.
  • Best Starter Pokémon: Pikachu, the iconic Electric-type Pokémon, evolves from Pichu and can further evolve into Raichu or Alolan Raichu.
