Fire Type Pokemon


  • Description: Fire-type Pokémon embody the element of fire, often boasting fiery designs and moves. These Pokémon are known for their aggressive and powerful attacks, frequently involving flames and heat-based techniques.
  • Strength: Fire types are super effective against Bug, Grass, Ice, and Steel Pokémon, making them formidable in battles against these types.
  • Weakness: They are, however, vulnerable to Water, Rock, and Ground types, as water extinguishes fire and rock and ground can withstand heat.
  • Best Starter Pokémon: Charmander, with its flame-tipped tail, evolves into Charizard, a powerful Fire/Flying-type Pokémon.
