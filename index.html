Pokédex

The Pokédex, created by Professor Oak, is a vital tool for Pokémon Trainers. This electronic device catalogs and provides essential information about various Pokémon species. With a sleek design and digital interface, it updates automatically as Trainers capture new Pokémon, offering valuable insights for battles and enriching the Trainer's journey of exploration and discovery in the Pokémon world.
