Water Type Pokemon


  • Description: Water-type Pokémon are often aquatic creatures with moves associated with water and ice. They are known for their versatility, excelling in both offense and defense.
  • Strength: Water types are strong against Fire, Ground, and Rock Pokémon, using their aquatic prowess to douse flames and overcome rock-based defenses.
  • Weakness: However, they are weak to Electric and Grass types, as electricity conducts well in water and plants absorb water for nourishment.
  • Best Starter Pokémon: Squirtle, a turtle-like Pokémon, evolves into Blastoise, a formidable Water-type with powerful water-based attacks.
